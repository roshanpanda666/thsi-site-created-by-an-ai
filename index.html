Captain America is a superhero from Marvel Comics. He was created by Joe Simon and Jack Kirby, and first appeared in Captain America Comics #1 in 1941. Captain America is a symbol of American patriotism and is known for his shield, which is made of vibranium and can deflect bullets and other projectiles.

Iron Man is a superhero from Marvel Comics. He was created by Stan Lee, Larry Lieber, Don Heck, and Jack Kirby, and first appeared in Tales of Suspense #39 in 1963. Iron Man is known for his high-tech suit of armor that he uses to fight crime and protect the world. He is also a billionaire industrialist and genius inventor.
